Privacy Policy

How Touch Abroad Educational Services collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Home›Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Touch Abroad Educational Services ("Touch Abroad", "we", "us" or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and the choices you have. We handle personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Ontario privacy legislation.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry form, call us or email us, we may collect:

  • Contact details such as your full name, phone number and email address;
  • Your program of interest and preferred method of contact;
  • Any information you choose to include in your message;
  • Technical data such as device type, browser, referring page and general location, collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools;
  • Marketing attribution data such as UTM parameters and ad-click identifiers, where present.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide guidance on programs and funding;
  • Assess eligibility and assist with college applications and enrolment;
  • Communicate with you using your preferred contact method;
  • Improve our website, services and marketing;
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

Consent

By submitting a form on this website and checking the consent box, you agree to be contacted by Touch Abroad regarding programs and funding. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us using the details below. Withdrawing consent will not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before you withdrew it.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies, including Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager and advertising pixels, to understand how visitors use our site and to measure the performance of our marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted partner colleges and service providers strictly as needed to process your enquiry, deliver our services or comply with the law. Any third parties we work with are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We apply reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect your information against loss, theft and unauthorised access.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request corrections, and ask us to delete it. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Phone: +1 (289) 969-7018
  • Email: info@touchabroad.ca
  • Address: 7560 Airport Rd #12, Mississauga, ON L4T 4H4

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
