Self-Funded PSW — FAQs

Everything international students and work permit holders need to know.

It's a comprehensive Personal Support Worker program at a government-recognised Ontario college, combining classroom theory with hands-on practical training. It's designed for international students and work permit holders and is paid through flexible, interest-free payment plans rather than government funding.
The Government-Funded PSW is for eligible Ontario Permanent Residents and Citizens who qualify for government education funding (potentially $0 tuition). The Self-Funded PSW is open to international students and work permit holders and is paid through interest-free instalments — so more people can start, regardless of funding eligibility.
Touch Abroad manages the entire admission process to our partner Canadian colleges for you. Just call +1 (289) 969-7018 or submit the enquiry form and our team will guide you from application through to enrolment.
