FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Touch Abroad programs and services.

Touch Abroad supports Work Permit Holders, Permanent Residents, Canadian Citizens and Protected Refugees who are living in Ontario and looking to start or advance their careers through education.
We offer five career-focused programs: Personal Support Worker (PSW), Office Administration Professional, Business Administration – Accounting & Finance, Cyber Security, and Early Childhood Assistant (ECA).
Government education funding, grants and student-loan support may be available for eligible Permanent Residents and Canadian Citizens in Ontario. Work Permit Holders and others may have access to self-funded options. Call us to check your specific eligibility.
No prior experience is required for any of our programs. They are all designed to be beginner-friendly and build your skills from the ground up, making them perfect for career changers and newcomers to Canada.
We provide complete application support — from selecting the right program and college to submitting your application and completing final enrolment. We manage the entire process so you don't have to navigate it alone.
Simply call us at +1 (289) 969-7018 or fill in the enquiry form on this page. Our team will reach out within 1 business hour to discuss your goals, check your eligibility and guide you through the next steps — completely free.
