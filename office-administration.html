About the Program

The Skills That Keep Every Business Running

Office administrators are the backbone of every organisation. This one-year Ontario College Certificate builds the technical and communication skills employers look for — from business writing and bookkeeping to advanced Microsoft Office tools.

It's also a recognised pathway: graduates can progress to a two-year Ontario College Diploma in Office Administration — Executive or Health Services. Touch Abroad handles funding and enrolment so you can focus on your future.

Office Administration — FAQs

Everything you need to know about the Office Administration Professional program.

A one-year Ontario College Certificate that teaches the technical, organisational and communication skills needed to work as an office administrator, receptionist or administrative assistant across any industry in Ontario.
